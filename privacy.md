# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

## 1. Introduction

Scantiv ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how we handle it when you purchase a GEO audit from us.

## 2. What We Collect

### Contact and delivery information

When you purchase a GEO audit, we collect your email address to deliver your report.

### Intake form data

After payment, you complete a short intake form. This asks for your website URL and basic details about your business. We use this information solely to produce your audit report.

### Payment information

Payments are processed by a third-party payment provider. We do not store your card number or payment credentials on our servers.

### Website analytics

We collect standard analytics data — page views, browser type, device type, and approximate location. This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Deliver your GEO audit report
- Process your payment
- Respond to support requests
- Improve our audit methodology using anonymised, aggregated data only

We do not sell your data. We do not use your information for advertising.

## 4. Data Sharing

We work with third-party providers for payment processing and analytics. These providers process data under their own privacy policies. We use only trusted providers that maintain appropriate security standards.

Any sample reports we use for illustration are fully anonymised. All identifying information is removed before use.

## 5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit and at rest, and controlled access to stored data.

## 6. Data Retention

- **Email and report data:** Retained for 12 months after your report is delivered, then deleted.
- **Payment records:** Retained for 7 years to meet legal and tax requirements.
- **Analytics data:** Retained for up to 2 years.

You may request early deletion at any time by contacting us.

## 7. Your Rights

Under Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA), you have the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- Withdraw consent and request deletion of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact@scantiv.com. We will respond within 30 days.

## 8. Cookies

We use cookies for basic analytics and session management. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect your experience on the site.

## 9. Children

Our service is intended for business owners and is not directed at anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by email where we have your contact details. Your continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## 11. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact us at contact@scantiv.com. We will respond within 30 days.
